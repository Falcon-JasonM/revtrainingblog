The first thing you will need to do to take your website live after you've built it is to get a static IP
  • The address you get from your internet provider is dynamic, and changes.
  • If you want people to be able to find their way to your site, it has to 'live' at a fixed, or static address.
  • You can get a free static web address from NoIP.com
  • You will need to set up port forwarding on your local router for this to work.
    Exact procedure depends on your ISP and router model.
    Suggest googling it for instructions.
